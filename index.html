Course 1: Discovering Your Purpose

The path to discovering your calling should be just as exciting as living it out. Sometimes we focus so much on arriving that we forget to simply enjoy the journey. This course will provide you with the practical steps to appreciating the process.

Course 2: You Are What You Think: Metacognition

It's so important to be aware of what you're thinking. The way we think about a situation, place, person or thing impact our interactions. The key to shifting what you think is to target how you think. This course will provide you with the necessary tools to monitor and assess your thoughts, feelings and reasoning.

Course 3: Watch Your Words: The Power of Speech

What you say is just as important as how you say it. You reap what you sow is not just pertaining to actions but words as well. The words you speak have the power to shape your experience whether positive or negative. This course examines the importance of being intentional about the words we release and where we place our agreement.

Course 4: An Authentic Image

Image is more than how you outwardly appear. How you show up internally impacts how you show up in the world. Although style has a part to play in a person's image, each person brings who they are to what they wear. This course examines the introspective nature of image and personal style.
